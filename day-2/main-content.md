# C.F.I.T.S

**C – Context**  
Provide the background, constraints, and relevant information the model needs.

**F – Format**  
Specify the required output structure (e.g., JSON, bullet points, code).

**I – Instructions**  
Define the task explicitly (what to do, not just the topic).

**T – Tone (or Type)**  
Control style, voice, or perspective (e.g., technical, legal, concise).

**S – Specificity (or Scope)**  
Constrain the response with limits (length, depth, assumptions, exclusions).
